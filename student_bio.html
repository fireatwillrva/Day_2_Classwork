Student Bio

Will Fisher

Will Fisher is an internationally published, award winning photographer and multi-instrumentalist residing in Richmond, VA with a Bachelor of Arts in Music (Specification in Audio Engineering and Digital Recording) and a Minor in Creative Advertising. In 2006, he founded Fire At Will Photography. Since its inception, Will has been published in the US, UK and Russia by Rolling Stone, MSN, Buzzfeed and more. In 2015, he received the Mark of Excellence Award by the Society of Professional Journalists at the University of Maryland for best supporting photo in a publication.

In 2013, Will cofounded Greyskull Studios, a music recording studio based in Colonial Heights, Virginia. From 2015-2016, Will served as the Primary Educator at Longwood University's Center for the Visual Arts, where he expanded the ArtKids program, a special outreach program that teaches photography to gifted and at-risk fourth graders at Cumberland County Elementary School and Prince Edward County Elementary School.

In 2016, Will founded The Cold Collective, a multimedia group and artist community dedicated to bridging the gap between the fragmented and compartmentalized art and music scenes of central Virginia. Every month, The Cold Collective features local artists of all mediums, as well as hosts in-house music and video productions. Will currently teaches private music lessons on oboe, saxophone and piano at Music & Arts and in-home.
